Literature & Art Corner

A Collection of AAPI literature and media

The 1882 Foundation's Literature & Art Corner aims to broaden public awareness and appreciation of Asian American history and culture. American textbooks do not sufficiently document its past and present experiences, and consequently, they are mostly unknown. Sharing literature and art produced by this community will rewrite it into the narrative and may inspire and empower AAPI youth.
